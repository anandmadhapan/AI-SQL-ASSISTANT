# Sample Datasets

Two sample CSV files are provided to get started quickly.

## sales_sample.csv

30 rows of order data covering Jan–Apr 2024.

**Columns:** `order_id`, `customer_name`, `product`, `category`, `quantity`, `unit_price`, `total_revenue`, `order_date`, `region`, `is_returned`

**Try these questions:**
- "Show total revenue by customer"
- "Which product category had the highest sales?"
- "Show all returned orders"
- "What is the total revenue by month?"
- "Find the top 5 orders by total revenue"
- "How many orders came from each region?"

## employees_sample.csv

20 rows of employee data across multiple departments.

**Columns:** `employee_id`, `first_name`, `last_name`, `department`, `job_title`, `salary`, `hire_date`, `city`, `is_active`, `years_experience`

**Try these questions:**
- "Show average salary by department"
- "Who are the top 5 highest-paid employees?"
- "List all engineers hired after 2020"
- "How many employees are in each city?"
- "Show employees with more than 10 years experience"
- "What is the salary range in the Engineering department?"
